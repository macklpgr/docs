<Accordion title="What's included in each plan?">
  Each plan includes:

  * **Articles**: Create fully SEO-optimized content pieces (quantity varies by plan)

  * **Keyword Reports**: Research reports to identify ranking opportunities

  * **Article Optimizations**: Tools to improve existing content

  * **Unlimited Rewrites**: Refine your generated articles as needed

  * **Auto-Indexing**: Get your content indexed by search engines

  Higher-tier plans include additional benefits like domain whitelabeling, unlimited auto-indexing sites, and priority support.
</Accordion>

## Pay-as-you-go with credits

<Note>
  Every new Byword account comes with 5 free article credits upon signup, so you can try the platform before committing to a plan.
</Note>

Don't need a full subscription? Byword offers a flexible credit system that lets you pay only for what you use.

<Info title="How Credits Work">
  &#x20; How do credits work?

  * Purchase credits anytime through your account dashboard

  * Each credit generates one article

  * Credits never expire

  * Ideal for occasional content needs or testing the platform
</Info>

While pay-as-you-go provides flexibility, subscription plans offer significant savings:

## FAQ

<Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?">
  Yes! You can change your plan at any time from your account settings. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to your new plan's features and article allowance. When downgrading, the change will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens if I use all my monthly articles?">
  If you reach your monthly article limit, you have two options:

  1. Purchase individual credits to create additional articles

  2. Wait until your plan refreshes at the start of your next billing cycle

  Any unused articles don't roll over to the next month.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are there any long-term commitments?">
  No, all Byword plans are billed monthly with no long-term commitment. You can cancel anytime from your account settings.
</Accordion>
